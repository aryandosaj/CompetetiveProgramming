Hungry Bhalu is climbing a Graph.
Every edge of the graph has a fruit on which a number is written.
While climbing an edge, bhalu is eating the fruit.
Bhalu will eat the fruit iff the fruits are in increasing order.
Bhalu can not skip a fruit.
Bhalu wants to have k number of fruits.
Check if bhalu can have k fruits.
INPUT
n, m, k: number of vertices and edges and k.
next m lines contain
u, v, x: there is an edge from u to v with number x 
Output
Print "YES" if bhalu can have k fruits else print "NO"

INPUT
6 7 5
1 2 1
3 2 5
2 4 2
2 5 2
2 6 9
5 4 3
4 3 4

Output 
YES
